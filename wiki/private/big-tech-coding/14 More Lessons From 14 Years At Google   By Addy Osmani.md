9. Make observability part of the feature.

11. When you add a team, you add edges, not just nodes.

12. The migration is never just a migration. Every migration is a negotiation.

The new one and the old one refuse to die.

The technical plan is the easy part. The hard part is designing for coexistence. You will need a adoption strategy that doesn’t require every team to drop what they’re doing at once.
